Mustard Vinaigrette

Description

This spicy honey-mustard dressing is an easy and versatile dressing for almost any salad!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 drops hot sauce

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the vinegar, honey, mustard, pepper, salt, garlic, oil and hot sauce until thoroughly combined. Chill until serving.
