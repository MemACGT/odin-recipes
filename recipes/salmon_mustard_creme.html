Salmon with Mustard-Creme Sauce

Description

In this super quick preparation, salmon fillets are brushed with Dijon mustard and quickly grilled, then served with a creamy mustard sauce with herbs and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 1 tub Philly Herb & Garlic Cooking Creme Cream Cheese Product
  • 1/2 cup 25% less sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoons grainy Dijon mustard
  • 6 salmon fillets

Directions

  1. Heat greased barbecue to medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir cooking creme, broth and 1 Tbsp. mustard in saucepan on medium heat 3 min. or until heated through. Remove from heat; cover to keep warm.
  3. Brush fish with remaining mustard; grill 2 to 3 min. on each side or until fish flakes easily with fork. Serve with warm sauce.
